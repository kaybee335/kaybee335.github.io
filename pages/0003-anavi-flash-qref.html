Serial flash Quick Reference for Anavi ESP8266 esp12e

Summary: This a quick reference to the flash procedure for development boars that are in use. It also lists the appropriate ESPHome platform/board. It assumes you know how to connect a UART to the board (but identifies connectors/pin locations/need for serial X-over) as well as prepare a firmware image for flashing.

Anavi ESP8266 esp12e

ESPHome: ESP8266 and esp12e

3.3VDC logic level UART, RX-TX crossover required, no additional signal pins (no full reset over serial after flash).

  1. Connect UART to unpowered board, power up while holding RESET button.
UART lead Anavi Pin Header
VIN 3.3V
GND GND
TX RX
RXD TX
  1. Start flash procedure and select appropriate COM port. Keep holding RESET switch.
  2. When flash completes, release RESET switch.
  3. Power cycle board to load/run new FW image.
